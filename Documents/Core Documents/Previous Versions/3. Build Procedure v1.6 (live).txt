8. Target, Challenge, Command – Object Behavior
From your clarified glossary and spec: [User Refernce Guide | Word], [Build Procedure 1 | Word], [First Conv...Copilot) 1 | Word]


Target:

Visual object (asteroid, zombie, ambulance, balloon, etc.).
Always has an attached Challenge; they move together as one unit.



Challenge:

Plain-language request describing what the player must do.
Rendered as a speech bubble or text positioned relative to the Target (above, below, left, right, depending on theme). [First Conv...Copilot) 1 | Word], [User Refernce Guide | Word]



Command:

Correct PowerLine syntax the player must type to clear the Target.
Uses the Command Template and Data Pools from the dataset. [User Refernce Guide | Word], [Build Procedure 1 | Word]



Hit / Miss / Impact:

Hit (Clear): correct Command; Target is removed.
Miss: incorrect Command; Target remains; shield may be lost.
Impact: Target reaches the impact zone without being cleared. [User Refernce Guide | Word], [Build Procedure 1 | Word]


9. Visual Motion & Glow (Themed Targets)
When a cartridge introduces rotating, glowing Targets (e.g., asteroid theme): [Build Procedure 1 | Word], [First Conv...Copilot) 1 | Word]


On spawn for each Target:

rotationSpeed = randomFloat(-2.0, 2.0)
fallSpeed = abs(rotationSpeed) + randomFloat(0.2, 0.5)



Per frame:

target.rotation += rotationSpeed
target.y       += fallSpeed



Glow:

Must follow the actual silhouette of the PNG, not its bounding box.
Must rotate with the object and stay aligned to its edges. [Build Procedure 1 | Word], [User Refernce Guide | Word], [First Conv...Copilot) 1 | Word]



Build Procedure requirement:

This behavior lives in the cartridge’s theme logic (e.g., asteroid-command/script.js), not in the core engine, to keep the Empty Cartridge neutral. [Build Procedure 1 | Word], [1. Laws v1.6 1 | Word], [<File>Buil...ocx</File> | Word]
