5. Passive Destruction Mode (NEW RULE)
(This is the new mechanic you just added.)
If the Command Tower reaches 0 health before all Zones are destroyed:
What happens:


Player input is disabled.

They cannot enter commands because the radio tower is destroyed.



Targets continue to spawn normally, aimed at remaining Zones.


Zones are destroyed one by one as asteroids impact them.


Game only ends when all Zones have been destroyed, even though the player cannot act.


This is a “forced loss cascade” sequence but:

It stays within the Impact Zone → ZoneDestroyed logic
It doesn’t violate your core architecture
It is purely cartridge behavior (not engine changes)

Everything in this section is grounded in your instruction inside Untitled.txt:

“If the tower gets destroyed before all the zones, I want the gameplay to continue.
The player will not be able to enter commands, and must watch the remaining zones get destroyed.”

6. Game-Over Conditions
There are two ways the game ends:
A. Tower destroyed after all Zones are gone

TowerHealth == 0
AND ZonesNorth/South/East all destroyed
→ Immediate Game Over

B. Tower destroyed before all Zones

TowerHealth == 0
Zones remain
→ Enter Passive Destruction Mode
→ Game Over occurs only when all Zones are destroyed
