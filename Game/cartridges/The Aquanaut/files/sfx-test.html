AUDIO: not started — click a cue
Audition a sample: drag a .wav/.mp3 onto any cue (or click ↥ sample). It plays in place of the synth immediately — click the cue to hear it, ↺ synth to A/B against the original. Loaded files live only in this page until you commit them.
Make it permanent (in the game): 1) save the file to files/assets/sfx/ · 2) click ⧉ spec to copy that cue's line · 3) paste it into AudioManager.sampleSpecs in core/audio.js. Missing files fall back to the synth, so it's safe to add them one at a time.
Notes: samples override the synth in Standard mode only (Holodeck stays digital). Leave fast cues like typing/fire on the synth — repeated samples sound machine-gunned. BURST ×6 replays a cue 6× to test rapid-fire feel.
